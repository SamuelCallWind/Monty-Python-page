Welcome to the round table

Welcome, you gallant gaggle of gigglers! Gather around our cherished, yet slightly worn-out Round Table. Take a seat, but keep thy wits about you - the chairs are as trustworthy as a minstrel's tale. Here, every voice counts, even Sir Bedevere's, who insists that ducks and coconuts share mysterious properties. So, strap in, speak up, and let's venture forth into the wild realms of rampant, rollicking absurdity! Let the funniness ensue!
